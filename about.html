About Me

My name is Ricardo Moritz Martinez and I am a software engineer from Miami, FL. In May 2017, I graduated from Columbia University's Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science with a B.S. in Computer Science. The following year, I started working as a software engineer for Tradebyte Software GmbH in Ansbach, Germany. At the end of June, 2020, in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, I returned home to my family in the U.S. In January 2021, I began working remotely for Mediaocean, Inc.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing and watching sports (particularly football, basketball, and soccer), and I like running late at night for exercise. When I'm inside, I'll usually be on the computer reading about and experimenting with any number of technical topics that, in some way, involve open-source or linux. I also have dual American/German citizenship (as my work experience in Germany may have hinted at), and regularly stay in touch with and try to visit my grandparents and uncle in Hamburg, Germany.

After having lived in a variety of different places throughout my life (Miami, New York City, Pittsburgh, Ansbach), I would say that I am always open to new experiencing new things and places, always trying to leave my mark!
